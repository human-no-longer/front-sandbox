Easy Steamed Green Beans

The perfect no-brainer side dish

Ingredients

  • Water, 1/4 cup
  • Salt, 1/4 tsp
  • Green beans, 1 cup, pieces
  • Oil, optional

Instructions

  1. Bring salted water to boil in a large frying pan or sauté pan.
  2. Add green beans, cover.
  3. Cook until green beans are tender to the bite and water has evaporated.
  4. Serve hot and enjoy!
