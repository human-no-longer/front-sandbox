Come to the Groundhog Day!

Groundhog Day is a tradition observed regionally in the United States and Canada on February 2 of every year

The Legend

According to folklore originating from German immigrants, if the animal emerges from its burrow on a sunny day and sees its shadow, it predicts six more weeks of winter. If it is cloudy and no shadow is seen, an early spring is expected.

Notable Beavers

  • Filbert the Beaver, also known as Stumptown Fil, is the official weather forecaster at the Oregon Zoo.
  • The Napanee Beaver is a local prognosticator in Ontario, Canada.
